Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Book Tracker & Recommendation app is to allow users to track the books they have read, are currently reading, and want to read. It also provides personalized book recommendations based on user ratings and preferences.

Audience

Avid readers, book lovers, and anyone interested in discovering and tracking books.

Dynamic elements

Js interact with their reading list, the app will dynamically update book statuses and show new suggestions based on user preferences.
